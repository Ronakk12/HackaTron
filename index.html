ABOUT HackaTron

An innovative event that blends students, technologists, academicians to ideate, formulate and render automotive concepts embodying the future of connected cars. HacamotiVE serves as a blithesome, explorative platform nurturing originative ideas affirming innovation. An opportunity for application pitching, idea transformation, realization with competent teams for building, delivering prototypes, consequently presented and evaluated by the mentors. Hackathon winners will be credited with astonishing rewards along with a chance to intern in latest tools and technologies trending automotive companies.


ABOUT CU

Chandigarh University, is a leading Indian Institution offering its students a unique amalgamation of professional and academic excellence. The University has been accredited with the prestigious A+ grade by the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC). Chandigarh University has become Youngest and the only private university in India to bag an A+ grade in the first cycle of the accreditation process and has also become the only state private university of Punjab to be accredited by NAAC. Also, ranked among Asia’s best and fastest-growing universities, CU has coupled the experience of top industry leaders and renowned academicians to foster a global approach. A wide spectrum of programs paired with flexibility, experiential learning and interdisciplinary orientation emancipate our students to explore their interests and pursue dream careers. At CU we are grooming students to be socially sensitive through intellectually challenging and contemporary diverse culture.

FAQ
You will work in team of max 5 persons to implement the solution that fits according to the problem statement you have chosen. Shortlisted teams would be prompted for the solution implementation and would be provided with 5 weeks of time to implement their ideas. The shortlisted teams on the last day will pitch at the finale of the HackaTron to present their projects (the projects will uploaded to youtube and the link should be shared).
All under and post graduate students irrespective of branch and year, who are registered under affiliated institutes across India. Reimbursements will not be provided.
Teams must consist of 1 to 5 members.
We recommend that you have a laptop/desktop to hack. If you have a certain set of tools (preferably open source) that you like to use, please feel free to use them as well.
The last date to submit your idea is 23rd of July.
This event is completely free for Cuians and Non-Cuians.
